Projects Requiring Funding

A number of Heyu users expressed interest in having support for Oregon Scientific weather instruments - remote Wind and Rain sensors - incorporated into Heyu.

Donations were solicited to enable purchase of an Oregon Scientific WMR80A Weather Station for development purposes.   The fundraising was successful and this project is now fully funded.   Many thanks to all who contributed.

There are no other projects requiring funding at this time.
